The [Wireless Community Weekend](https://wiki.freifunk.net/Wireless_Community_Weekend_2026) will follow the more than 20 years of tradition also in 2026 and takes place at the c-base space station in Berlin. From the 15th to 17th of May the Freifunk community will meet with their guests to create an unconference and hackathon.
