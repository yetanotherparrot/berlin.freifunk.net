On Community Day, we come together to discuss important topics in the Berlin Freifunk network. On this day, we take the time to address larger issues that cannot easily be resolved via chat. Between the recaps and outlooks, there will be warm food and snacks. To ensure we can discuss the topics effectively, the preparation group has compiled an [agenda with a schedule](https://github.com/freifunk-berlin/meta/issues/176). Afterwards, there will still be plenty of time for casual exchanges.

**Location notes:**

- S-Bahn station Petershagen Nord
- Bicycle and car parking available
